Terms Of Service

This platform should only be used to review businesses based on their covid-19 security measures. The main focus of this site to give shop owners feedback about how secure their customers feel. Make sure that when writing a review, you give clear arguments about what is good and what needs to be improved.
This platform should by no means be used to harass businesses.

For basic guidelines businesses need to follow: https://www.who.int/docs/default-source/coronaviruse/getting-workplace-ready-for-covid-19.pdf

Noticed abuse of this TOS? Please contact us at SafetyAdvisor@protonmail.com.
